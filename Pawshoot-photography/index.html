Photoshoots for pets - made easy.

01

First check out your calendar and book a time with us!

Pawshoot has made it possible for you to book your time through pc and mobile! Simple design allows you to choose a date, location, session length and even the photographer of your preference. Each of our photographers have written their own biography for you to discover via double clicking on their image. Take your time finding the one you think you'd spend the most amazing time with!

02

Easy to fill contact information

The process is no more difficult than this! Fill out your typical contact information so we get to know something about you too!

By subscribing to our newsletter gives you the privilege to see few of the other pets' photoshoot pictures every couple weeks, and we hold little competitions between these images, where winners get even more photoshoots - for free!

03

Order confirmation

Our order confirmation will be displayed at the end of your order and will be both sent in your and the photographer's email! We want to be absolutely sure you (and us) will remember what we've agreed on. That's about it, we can't wait to see you and your adorable pets!
